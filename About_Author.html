About the Author

Ayushman Jena

Ayushman Jena is an electrical engineering graduate from VSSUT Burla, a passionate space-tech enthusiast, and a Junior Research Fellow at NIT Trichy. His journey has been marked by significant projects, including participation in the CANSAT competition and the World Space Week 2023, where he presented an innovative CubeSat design for RF energy harvesting in space. Ayushman has also gained experience through a DRDO internship, focusing on embedded systems and IoT, and contributes to experimental rocketry through the VSLV 4.0 rocket project, named 'Chroma-Krut,' at the Veer Surendra Space Innovation Center.

In addition to his technical pursuits, Ayushman is deeply interested in contributing to advancements in space technology, with an emphasis on sustainable and innovative designs for outer space exploration and energy harvesting solutions. His work reflects a blend of creativity and technical skill, aimed at solving real-world problems.
